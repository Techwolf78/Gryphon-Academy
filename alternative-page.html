What We Do

Brainstorm

Every project starts with a brain storming session with our team of designers and developers with the project's top stake holders. We dont start building till we know the 'why' of the project and your vision.

Design & Develop

Once the 'why' has been established we get working on the 'how'. Now we have our guidelines and with wireframes, mockups, builds and iteration, the team gets crackin' and you get regular updates.

Quality Assurance

A site is not complete till its functional on ALL modern browsers and devices. We do rigorous QA testing to make sure that both you and your audience are satisfied. When you finally release its a masterpiece.

Ship it Out!

We don't ship sites until they are 100% ready to go. Once all the testing is done we cross our fingers and release to the wild. If you love something, let it go. If it doesn't come back then we did our job right!
